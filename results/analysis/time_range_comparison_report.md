## 关键差异

- 随着访问时间增长，平均聚类大小呈现增长趋势，说明用户行为更加集中或分散
- 短时间访问用户特有的关注点: Purchase platform (Amazon vs. Ikarao Store), After-Sale Service, Product not turning on, flashing red light, potential hardware failure, need for immediate replacement for an upcoming event.
- 长时间访问用户特有的关注点: product functionality and support, product usage, features, and app integration, Warranty information and product registration

## 趋势分析

- 关注点'product usage, charging, shipping, and potential issues like damage or incorrect items'在长时间访问用户中显著增加
